# Code of Conduct

We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community.

All contributors are expected to:

* Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
* Treat every contributor with kindness and respect.
* Provide constructive and respectful feedback.
* Foster a positive, collaborative, and inclusive environment.
* Respect differing opinions and experiences.

By participating in this project, you agree to follow these guidelines and help create a friendly and supportive community for everyone.
